Grammar Rules
📌 Регулярні дії, факти, звички, розкладу. Маркери: always, often, usually, every day, never, sometimes.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + V1 (he/she/it → V + s/es)
I work every day.
She works in an office.
They play football on Sundays.
⚠️ he/she/it: adds -s (play→plays), -es (go→goes, watch→watches), або -ies (study→studies)
✕ Negative
Subject + do/does + not + V1
I do not work on weekends.
She does not like coffee.
⚠️ he/she/it → does not (doesn't). Основне дієслово без -s!
? Question
Do/Does + subject + V1?
Do you speak English?
Does he work here?
📌 Завершені дії в минулому. Маркери: yesterday, last week, in 2020, ago, when.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + V2 (past form)
I worked yesterday.
She visited Paris last year.
He went to school. (irregular)
⚠️ Правильні: + -ed. Неправильні: окрема форма (go→went, see→saw, take→took)
✕ Negative
Subject + did not + V1
I did not work yesterday.
She did not go to the party.
⚠️ Після did/did not — завжди V1 (інфінітив без to)
? Question
Did + subject + V1?
Did you call me?
Did she finish the task?
📌 Спонтанні рішення, передбачення, обіцянки. Маркери: tomorrow, next week, soon, I think.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + will + V1
I will call you tomorrow.
She will help us.
✕ Negative
Subject + will not + V1
I will not be late.
They won't come tonight.
⚠️ won't = will not (скорочення)
? Question
Will + subject + V1?
Will you help me?
Will it rain tomorrow?
📌 Дія відбувається зараз або тимчасово. Маркери: now, right now, at the moment, currently, today, this week.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + am/is/are + V-ing
I am working right now.
She is reading a book.
They are playing football.
⚠️ I → am · he/she/it → is · you/we/they → are
✕ Negative
Subject + am/is/are not + V-ing
I am not sleeping.
He is not driving a car.
? Question
Am/Is/Are + subject + V-ing?
Are you listening to me?
Is she cooking dinner?
⚠️ Написання V-ing: відкритий склад з однією приголосною → подвоїти (run→running, sit→sitting). Кінцева -e → прибрати (make→making, come→coming).
📌 Дія тривала в конкретний момент у минулому або перервалась іншою. Маркери: at 5 o'clock, when, while, at that moment.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + was/were + V-ing
I was sleeping at ten.
We were waiting for you.
⚠️ I/he/she/it → was · you/we/they → were
✕ Negative
Subject + was/were not + V-ing
He was not studying yesterday.
They were not playing football.
? Question
Was/Were + subject + V-ing?
Were they playing football?
Was it raining at noon?
💡 While + Past Continuous / when + Past Simple: I was cooking when she called.
📌 Дія буде тривати в певний момент у майбутньому. Маркери: at this time tomorrow, at 5 pm, in an hour.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + will be + V-ing
I will be working at five.
They will be flying home.
✕ Negative
Subject + will not be + V-ing
We will not be driving.
She won't be sleeping.
? Question
Will + subject + be + V-ing?
Will you be using this?
Will he be waiting for us?
📌 Досвід, результат у теперішньому, дія нещодавно завершилась. Маркери: already, yet, just, ever, never, for, since, recently.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + have/has + V3
I have never been to Japan.
She has already finished the report.
⚠️ I/you/we/they → have · he/she/it → has. V3 = 3-тя форма (seen, finished, gone)
✕ Negative
Subject + have/has not + V3
We have not seen that film.
He has not called me yet.
? Question
Have/Has + subject + V3?
Have you ever tried sushi?
Has she passed the exam?
💡 already (стверджувальне) / yet (питальне/заперечне) / just (щойно) / ever (коли-небудь) / never (ніколи).
📌 Дія завершилась до іншої дії в минулому. Маркери: before, after, already, by the time, when (+ Past Simple).
✓ Affirmative
Subject + had + V3
I had already eaten when you called.
They had left before I arrived.
✕ Negative
Subject + had not + V3
She had not slept for two days.
? Question
Had + subject + V3?
Had you ever visited Rome before?
💡 Послідовність: дія 1 (Past Perfect) → дія 2 (Past Simple). She had finished her work before he arrived.
📌 Дія завершиться до певного моменту в майбутньому. Маркери: by tomorrow, by the time, by next year.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + will have + V3
I will have finished by 5 pm.
She will have left by the time you arrive.
✕ Negative
Subject + will not have + V3
We will not have arrived by then.
? Question
Will + subject + have + V3?
Will you have finished by noon?
📌 Дія почалась у минулому, триває й досі або щойно завершилась (і є видимий результат). Маркери: for, since, how long, all day, lately, recently.
✓ Affirmative
Subject + have/has been + V-ing
She has been learning English for two years.
I have been waiting here for an hour.
✕ Negative
Subject + have/has not been + V-ing
He has not been sleeping well lately.
? Question
Have/Has + subject + been + V-ing?
Have you been waiting long?
Has she been working here since June?
💡 for + тривалість (for 2 hours), since + початок (since Monday). Акцент на тривалості процесу, а не на результаті.
